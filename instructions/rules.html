Rules

A cursed batch of cookies has been baked.

Somewhere in the cookie jar lurks a Crumbling Cookie — and if you draw one, you're out... unless you have a Milk* Splash to save yourself.

Use spells, frogs, vinyl records and sheer dice-rolling luck to outlast everyone else.

*This is a Derry-free product, good for vegans and lactose intolerant people, dreaded by everyone else

Base game

2-3 Players • Ages 10+ • 15-30 min

Setup

  1. Remove all Crumbling Cookies and Milk Splashes from the deck.
  2. Deal four cards to each player. Then deal one Milk Splash to each player, so everyone has 5 cards each.
  3. Shuffle the Crumbling Cookies back into the deck. Make sure there’s one Crumbling Cookies card less than the number of players. (e.g. two players → one Crumbling Cookies)
  4. You may have leftover Milk Splashes. Shuffle the extras back into the deck.
  5. Shuffle the deck.
  6. The person who most recently ate something sweet goes first*.
* Biting Sugar may only be done with his consent.

How to Play

On your turn, you may optionally play any number of action cards from your hand.

Your turn ends when you or another player draws a card from the deck or a card effect ends your turn earlier.

If an action causes another player to draw card(s), your turn ends and it is now the attacked player’s turn.

Drew a Crumbling Cookie? Play a Milk Splash to survive - then secretly reinsert the cookie anywhere in the deck. No Milk Splash? Your cookie crumbles and you're eliminated.

Multi-card draws are limited by the number of cards remaining in the deck.

The last intact cookie wins! Glory, crumbs, and bragging rights are yours.

While the public mocks the shattered remains of a dry biscuit, a heroic deluge of dairy arrives to save the lord’s dignity-leaving the jar to simmer in a silent, ceramic rage.
