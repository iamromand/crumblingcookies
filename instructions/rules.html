Rules

A cursed batch of cookies has been baked.

Somewhere in the cookie jar lurks a Crumbling Cookie - and if you draw one, you're out... unless you have a Milk* Splash to save yourself.

Use spells, frogs, vinyl records and sheer dice-rolling luck to outlast everyone else.

*This is a Derry-free product, good for vegans and lactose intolerant people, dreaded by everyone else

Base game

2-3 Players • Ages 10+ • 15-30 min

Setup

  1. Remove all Crumbling Cookie cards and Milk Splash cards from the deck.
  2. Deal four cards to each player. Then deal one Milk Splash to each player, so everyone has 5 cards each.
  3. Shuffle the Crumbling Cookie cards back into the deck. Make sure there’s one Crumbling Cookie card less than the number of players. (e.g. two players → one Crumbling Cookie)
  4. You may have leftover Milk Splash card. Shuffle the extras back into the deck.
  5. Shuffle the deck.
  6. The person who most recently ate something sweet goes first*.
* Biting Sugar may only be done with his consent.

How to Play

On your turn, you may optionally play any number of action cards from your hand.

Your turn ends when you or another player draws a card from the deck or a card effect ends your turn earlier.

If an action causes another player to draw card(s), your turn ends and it is now the attacked player’s turn.

Drew a Crumbling Cookie? Play a Milk Splash to survive - then secretly reinsert the cookie anywhere in the deck. No Milk Splash? Your cookie crumbles and you're eliminated.

Multi-card draws are limited by the number of cards remaining in the deck.

The last intact cookie wins! Glory, crumbs, and bragging rights are yours.

While the public mocks the shattered remains of a dry biscuit, a heroic deluge of dairy arrives to save the lord’s dignity-leaving the jar to simmer in a silent, ceramic rage.

The cookie jar grows hungrier. The cookies grow crumblier. You invited more friends - and now they're all going to suffer alongside you. Shuffle in the expansion, prep the die, and pray the jar shows mercy.

Tempt the Jar

The jar knows what you want. It also knows how to hurt you.

Once per turn, before drawing or playing an action that ends the turn, any player may choose to Tempt the Jar - but only if the discard pile contains one or more Milk Splash cards.

Roll the die and compare your result to the number of Milk Splash cards currently in the discard pile:

Die Result in relation to Milk Splash cards in the discard pile Effect
Result is lower than no. of Milk Splash cards Draw two cards (one at a time)
Result is equal than no. of Milk Splash cards Take one Milk Splash from the discard pile into your hand
Result is higher than no. of Milk Splash cards Skip your draw phase AND discard one card from your hand

This ends your turn.

Keep Milk Splash cards in a separate discard pile, slightly spread out so all players can count them at a glance.

If you draw two Crumbling Cookie cards, you will need two Milk Splash cards.
