Jar Bait

There are those who tempt the jar of their own free will. Then there are those who are pushed. Both are equally doomed.

Play on any other player That player must immediately Tempt the Jar - rolling the die and resolving the Cookie Jar Roll effect. This ends your turn. Game resumes from the player who should have followed you.
⚠️ The discard pile minimum of one Milk Splash cards still applies. If the pile is too small, this card is wasted.
