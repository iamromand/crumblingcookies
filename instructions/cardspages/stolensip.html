Stolen Sip

Pinching another's milk - a crime punishable, in the old tongue, by a very stern look and possible exile from the biscuit tin.

Point to a specific card in another player’s hand. They must show that card to you. If it's a Milk Splash, take it into your hand. If it's anything else, nothing happens. Shield Spell can protect the chosen player from showing and giving you the card.
