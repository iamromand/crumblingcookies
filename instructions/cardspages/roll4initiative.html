Roll 4 Initiative

The die was cast. The crowd fell silent. Someone's aunt fainted. Nobody drew a four.

Roll the die and consult the result:

1-3 Skip your draw phase entirely.
4 Draw up to 3 cards yourself.
5-6 Force any one player to draw up to 2 cards.

This ends your turn(s) and play continues with the person following the person drawing.
